01

Structured Settlements

Structured settlements provide injury victims with a stream of tax‑advantaged periodic payments. Rather than receiving a lump sum, you receive guaranteed payments funded by highly rated life insurance companies, helping to cover medical expenses, lost income and future needs while protecting your eligibility for government benefits.

02

Complimentary Products

In addition to structured settlements, we offer other settlement-planning options-such as trust services, Medicare set-aside arrangements and special-needs planning-to ensure that every aspect of your financial future is considered.

03

Fee Deferrals for Attorneys

Contingency-fee attorneys can defer some or all of their fees and receive periodic payments over time. By structuring fees, you manage income peaks and valleys and defer income tax until the payments are received. Ringler consultants work with your tax advisor to set up compliant fee-deferral plans

Structured Instalment Sales

05

Our Stance On Factoring

Ringler does not purchase structured settlement payments. We believe in protecting the financial security of injured people and strongly condemn predatory factoring practices that offer lump sums at steep discounts. If you need liquidity from your settlement, our consultants can discuss safer alternatives and help you understand the long-term impact of selling your payments
